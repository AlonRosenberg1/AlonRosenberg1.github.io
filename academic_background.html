Alon Rosenberg - Academic background

I hold B.Sc and M.Sc in electrical engineering, and a B.Sc in physics.
During those degrees I took advanced courses in machine learning, computer vision and signal processing.
Some of the noteable subjects I encountered in my academic experience are:

Machine learning.

Bayesian reasoning, SVM, regression, neural networks, decision trees, clustering, boosting, overfitting, 3 sets split, learning theory.

Computer vision

2D DFT, noise filtering (non local means filter, diffusion methods, TV minimization), de convolution, segmentation (mean shift, active contours), registration, radon transform.

Signal processing.

MAP, MMSE and ML estimators, parameters estimation (for general signals and for ARMA process), fourier analysis, auto and cross correlation, power spectrum, kalman filter.

Digital signal processing (DSP).

Finite precision effects , sampling, DTFT and DFT, periodogram, multirate (up and down sample), time frequency analysis (wavelets, gabor transform and STFT).

Algorithms.

Short paths algorithms (Bellman Ford, Dijkstra, Johnson), dynamic programing, graph flow, linear programing, reductions and complexity families.

Communication.

AM and FM modulations, baseband analysis, error estimation, coding.
